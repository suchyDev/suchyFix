About suchyfix

suchyFiX is a free (and ad-free) application created by suchyDev, built on top of the OpenStreetMap and Thunderforest open map data,
as well as HERE WeGo and other services.

You can easily explore with different map layers available and fix your position with gps or reverse ip search.

No fees, no ads, just maps!

You can download suchyFix for android for free on the Amazon App store.
